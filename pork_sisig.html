Sizzling Pork Sisig

Pork Sisig - 10.00 AED

Pork sisig is a dish prepared using cut pork, mixing both lean besides fatty sections to produce a crisp, meaty feel.

Combined with calamansi, chili next to seasoning, it gives a salty, sour next to somewhat hot taste. Some versions include an egg or mayonnaise topping to increase creaminess.

Excellent as a topping for rice bowls or an appetizer, it is enjoyed because of its strong, tasty along with crisp flavor.
