Menu of the Week (Dishes)


Lumpiang Shanghai is a Filipino classic that never fails to impress in many parties!

These perfectly rolled spring rolls are packed with seasoned ground meat, finely chopped vegetables, and are fried to perfection!

This is a dish which may be eaten as an appetizer or as an ulam (main course) with rice!

It is a staple at Filipino gatherings. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a satisfying meal, this classic will always hit the spot.

Crispy Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai - 10.00 AED


Lumpiang Shanghai is typically served as a snack or appetizer, usually with a dipping sauce like sweet and sour sauce, ketchup, or spicy vinegar.

This dish consists of thin, delicate spring roll wrappers wrapped tightly around a flavorful filling of ground pork, carrots, onions, and, on occasion, shrimp or other vegetables.

To enhance the umami flavor, season the mixture with soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper.

The rolls are then deep-fried to a golden perfection, leaving a crunchy exterior and a juicy, flavorful filling inside.
