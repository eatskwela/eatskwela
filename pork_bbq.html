Menu of the Week (Snacks)


Filipino snacks, or merienda, are more than just quick bites. They are a treasured aspect of the culture that unite the Filipinos around basic yet tasty snacks.

These snacks perfectly capture the spirit of Filipino cuisine, whether they are handmade or sold on the street, sweet or savory.

Every taste of the smoky, grilled snacks, chewy kakanin, and even the crispy turons tells a story of comfort and tradition.

This week's menu features traditional merienda staple pork BBQ, providing comfort in school and reminiscent of the comfort food enjoyed in the Philippines!
