Welcome to Eatskwela


We aim to bring the warmth and comfort of Filipino home-cooked meals to students, ensuring that every bite feels like home—no matter where they are.

We are committed to serving delicious, nourishing, and authentic Filipino dishes that provide a sense of belonging and comfort,
especially to students who don’t bring lunch but deserve a taste of home!
