Appetizing Bistek

Beef Bistek Tagalog - 10.00 AED


A cozy and savory dish. Thin slices of beef cooked in a fragrant mixture of soy sauce, calamansi juice, and onions make up this delectable Filipino meal.

Served with warm white rice, Bistek Tagalog is simple, hearty, and full of comforting flavors that make every bite satisfying.

The onions are sautéed and added near the end, providing a nice balance of sweetness and texture.
